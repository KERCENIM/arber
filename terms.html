Please read this before submitting. Arbërikon collects voluntary Y-DNA information from people of Albanian descent to build an open map of Albanian paternal lineages. By submitting the form, you agree to the terms below. If you do not agree, please do not submit. This document is written in plain language; it is not legal advice, and it does not create any professional relationship between you and Arbërikon.

1. Who We Are

Arbërikon is an independent, non-commercial heritage and research project documenting Albanian history, genetics, and linguistics. We are not a medical provider, a laboratory, an ancestry-testing company, or a government body. We are not affiliated with any DNA-testing service you may have used.

2. What You May Submit

Through the submission form you may voluntarily provide some or all of the following:

  • Your Y-DNA haplogroup, subclade / terminal SNP, and mtDNA haplogroup
  • A general place of paternal origin (district, region, country) and, optionally, fis / tribe
  • The testing company you used and free-text notes
  • Optionally, a raw DNA data file or a link to one
  • Optionally, a contact (email or social handle) if you wish to receive a reply

Please do not include your full legal name or other unnecessary identifying details. Only submit what you are comfortable sharing.

3. Eligibility

You must be 18 years of age or older to submit. Do not submit data belonging to a minor. By submitting, you confirm that you meet this age requirement.

4. Your Representations

By submitting, you confirm that:

  • The genetic data is your own, or you have the explicit authority and consent of the person it belongs to;
  • You have the right to share it, and doing so does not violate any agreement with your testing provider or any third party's rights;
  • The information you provide is accurate to the best of your knowledge.

5. How We Use Your Submission

We use submitted data to:

  • Verify and categorise Y-DNA haplogroups and subclades;
  • Plot lineages on the public Arbërikon Albanian Y-DNA map;
  • Conduct and share non-commercial research on Albanian population genetics and history.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, attempt to personally identify you, or share raw files with third parties.

6. What Becomes Public

Only non-identifying information appears on the public map — typically your haplogroup / subclade and a general geographic point (district or region level). Your raw data file, contact details, and any notes you provide are kept private and are not published. We aim to keep mapped points general enough that they cannot reasonably identify an individual.

7. Storage & Security

Submissions are received and stored through a third-party form service and are accessible only to the Arbërikon maintainer. While we take reasonable care to protect submissions, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Submit only what you are comfortable sharing under those terms.

8. Withdrawal & Deletion

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may request that your submission and any associated file be removed at any time, for any reason, by contacting us (see Section 12). We will delete it from our records and remove the corresponding point from the map within a reasonable period. To help us locate your entry, please include the details you originally submitted.
